    i-recruit | Permanent & Temporary Recruitment Agency

    WHO ARE WE?

    i-recruit is a recruitment agency that provides a customised, specialist and personal approach to staffing. If you’re finding that you just don’t have the time to manage your recruitment process, then the team here at i-recruit can help you out in a heartbeat.

    WHAT DO WE DO?

    We are experts in recruiting, meeting rigorous standards to bring you the best candidates in their field. We’re AS4801 Safety Certified and we devote ourselves to this commitment day in and day out. What’s more, we’re 100% Australian owned and managed, providing a “single source” staffing option for employers.

    Our services to bring you the right candidates are 24/7. If you’re seeking temporary staffing, contract personnel and permanent placements across Corporate, Civil or Construction, let us find you the right people.

    We understood that not all companies have the capacity to undergo a huge recruitment process, which is why we have been providing our services since 1999. Offering a highly personal approach with good quality service, along with technological and social media strategies, this ensures that the very best candidates and talent are just a phone call away. What’s more, our Recruitment Personnel have ready access to a wide variety of potential talent through:

    • Greater advertising reach;
    • A comprehensive database of former applicants; and,
    • A broader network of contacts developed over years working within the Recruitment Industry.

    We’ve got great people in Brisbane ready to start tomorrow.

    So contact us today on (07) 3868 3525. We'll find you the right people.
