    Legal and Accounting

    Are you seeking legal staff, but don’t have the time, staff or resources to manage a recruitment process to find suitable qualified people? Allow i-recruit to assist with your next legal placement 

    WHO ARE WE?

    We are a boutique, specialist recruitment agency featuring temporary and permanent recruiting across many different sectors and areas. With over 10 years experience in the recruitment industry, having been in business since 1999, we are also experienced and knowledgeable in the legal profession and will find you the best match for your available role.

    WHAT DO WE DO?

    Through a detailed system we source relevant, compatible candidates in their fields for employers looking to fill positions. We continue to provide successful staffing solutions to professional firms in Brisbane and the Gold Coast.

    HOW DO WE DO IT?

    Incorporating a rigorous and thorough check of candidates to ensure their legitimacy and compatibility, we ensure that we match suitable candidates with employers successfully time and time again. Background checks are completed, along with qualification verification and thorough screening processes. This ensures we source the most qualified and competent individuals in their fields for you and your business.
