    WHO IS i-recruit?

    i-recruit is an experienced, qualified and certified recruitment agency operating for more than 10 years. We understand that matching people to companies perfectly can be extremely time consuming. We take a personal and customised approach to recruitment, ensuring both employer and employee are matched suitably for both parties. 

    WHAT DO WE DO?

    We take a sophisticated and rigorous approach to our recruitment process. We ensure that people are thoroughly screened, including their qualifications, achievements and work history and we ensure that we match the most suitable person to every vacancy.

    HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

    We continue to strive to be one of the leading admin temp agencies in Brisbane by maintaining a strong focus on the things that really matter. We achieve this by providing you with tips, tricks and resume pointers to help you through the recruitment process and achieve employment as soon as possible. 

    We can help match you with a potential employer seeking your skill set and experience for a fulfilling career opportunity.
